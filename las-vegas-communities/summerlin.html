The Community

Life in Summerlin

Summerlin is one of the nation's top-selling master-planned communities, nestled against the stunning Spring Mountains and Red Rock Canyon on the western edge of Las Vegas. With sweeping views of the Las Vegas Valley and the Spring Mountains, this coveted enclave offers a lifestyle that seamlessly blends natural beauty with modern convenience.

Developed by The Howard Hughes Corporation, Summerlin features a diverse collection of single-family homes ranging from stylish townhomes to expansive estate residences — all within a meticulously planned environment designed for an elevated lifestyle at every stage.

Downtown Summerlin — an open-air shopping, dining and entertainment destination with 125+ stores
Over 150 miles of trails connecting to Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area
Top-rated schools including The Meadows School and numerous CCSD Blue Ribbon campuses
Walkable to Whole Foods, Trader Joe's, fine dining, and Downtown Summerlin entertainment
Easy access to I-215 and Highway 95 — 20 minutes to the Las Vegas Strip

Community Highlights

Explore the schools, amenities, recreation, and lifestyle features that make Summerlin one of the most desirable communities in Nevada.

Explore the Community

Schools

Anthem Highlands is served by some of Clark County's highest-rated schools. Elise L. Wolff Elementary and Del E. Webb Middle School are both within the community. Liberty High School and The Meadows School (private) are also within easy reach, making it an ideal choice for families prioritizing education.

Top-Rated CCSD Schools
Amenities

The Anthem Community Center — a 65,000 sq ft recreation facility — anchors the community's amenity offering. It features resort-style pools, tennis and pickleball courts, a state-of-the-art fitness center, group fitness classes, a skate park, and extensive youth programs. HOA-maintained landscaping is included throughout.

65,000 sq ft Rec Center
Parks & Recreation

Over 60 miles of walking, jogging, and cycling trails wind through Anthem Highlands and connect to the broader Anthem master plan. Cornerstone Park — one of Henderson's largest — is just minutes away with soccer fields, a children's splash pad, picnic areas, and a butterfly garden. Desert preserves provide scenic hiking terrain year-round.

60+ Miles of Trails
Shopping & Dining

Daily essentials are within a 5–10 minute drive: Trader Joe's, Whole Foods, Sprouts, Target, and Home Depot are all nearby along the Anthem Parkway corridor. For upscale dining and boutique retail, the District at Green Valley Ranch — just 15 minutes away — offers a walkable outdoor scene with wine bars, restaurants, and specialty shops.

Trader Joe's · Whole Foods Nearby
Lifestyle

Summerlin offers a rare combination of suburban tranquility and city convenience. With Red Rock Canyon as your backyard, world-class golf courses, and vibrant community events year-round, residents enjoy an active, connected lifestyle. Downtown Summerlin and the Strip are both within easy reach.

Guard-Gated · 20 Min to Strip
Nearby Attractions

Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area is minutes from your door — ideal for hiking, rock climbing, and scenic drives. TPC Summerlin hosts the PGA TOUR Shriners Hospitals Open. The Vegas Golden Knights and Las Vegas Aces home games, Allegiant Stadium, and the Strip's legendary entertainment are all under 30 minutes away.
